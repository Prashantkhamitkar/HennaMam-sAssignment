Koyasan Buddhist Temple

A Spiritual Journey Through Little Tokyo

Walk into Downtown LA's largest ethnic neighborhood of Little Tokyo and learn about the history of the Japanese people with growth, migration, World War II, and its aftermath. Find out more about how this community endured hardships and success while retaining a strong cultural identity.

Special Feature: Includes a guided meditation session at the temple

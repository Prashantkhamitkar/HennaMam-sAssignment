Wilshire Center & Koreatown Tour

Exploring Hollywood's Golden Age & Korean Heritage

In the early 1900s, Wilshire Center was the glittering hub of Hollywood's Golden Age social life and the new commercial and business heart of the City. Towards the end of the century, this section of Mid-Wilshire became the home of the largest Korean population in the United States. We'll visit iconic LA landmarks and remember our historical lost beauties on this marveling adventure through Koreatown!

Note: This tour can be hosted as a live walking tour or as a virtual experience.
